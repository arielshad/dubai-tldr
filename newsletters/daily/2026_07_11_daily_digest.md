<h2 style="margin: 0 0 8px; font-size: 24px; font-weight: 800; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.2;">Dubai waterfront homes +140% in five years — AED 9.05B luxury tape and 68 deals above $25M in 2025</h2>
<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">The five-year waterfront-premium receipt: +140% appreciation, four of five top Q4 2025 neighbourhoods coastal, and AED 9B of &gt;$10M luxury clearing in 2025.</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
Khaleej Times' 10 July 2026 read — framed around Shamal Holding, Knight Frank Mena and a YouGov study — prints +140% five-year appreciation for prime Dubai coastal homes and sizes the 2025 &gt;$10M luxury tape at AED 9.05B (+27.7% YoY) with 68 homes sold above $25M.
</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
The thesis is scarcity + lifestyle-ecosystem preference: Palm Jumeirah, Palm Jebel Ali, Dubai Islands, Emaar Beachfront and Jumeirah Bay Island are the finite supply, and four of the five top-performing Q4 2025 neighbourhoods are waterfront communities.
</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
The +140% five-year and AED 9.05B 2025 tape quantify the coastal premium for brokers and buy-side advisors — but they also flag the risk that the waterfront premium is already priced in at H1 2026 levels, so contrarian entries must look at second-line waterfront or off-plan pipeline.
</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Who is it for?</strong><br>
Luxury brokers pricing Palm / Islands / Beachfront stock, UHNW buyer advisors modelling waterfront-premium risk, and portfolio managers weighting waterfront vs inland Dubai.
</p>
