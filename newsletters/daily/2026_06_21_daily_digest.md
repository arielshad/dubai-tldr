<h2 style="margin: 0 0 8px; font-size: 24px; font-weight: 800; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.2;">Dubai Metro Gold Line repricing scan — 15–30% historic metro premium mapped across JVC, MBR City, Meydan and Business Bay</h2>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #8a8a85; line-height: 1.5;">Brokers and developers put a number on the Gold Line: five named houses anchor 15–30% historic metro-adjacency premium, with JVC, MBR City and Meydan as the first repricing winners.</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
Gulf News pulls five named brokers (HRE Development, Arabian Acres, Merlin, Meraki, Pride &amp; Property) into a single read on the AED 34B fully-underground Dubai Metro Gold Line (18 stations, Sept 2032) — JVC, MBR City, Meydan, Al Barsha South and Business Bay named as the corridor's first repricing winners, covering ~1.5M residents and 55 already-built developments.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">How does it work?</strong><br>
HRE Development puts the historic premium at 20–30% (18–25% during the build phase); interchanges lock in at Business Bay (Red Line), Al Ghubaiba (Green Line) and planned Etihad Rail at Meydan and Jumeirah Golf Estates. RTA models ~20% uplift in property values near stations and a 430% cumulative 20-year economic return.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Why does it matter?</strong><br>
The Gold Line starts from a stronger base than past greenfield corridors — JVC already runs Dubai's highest residential volumes, and Business Bay is one of the city's most liquid resale districts. The announcement-to-visible-construction window is historically the cleanest entry point; sellers in JVC / MBR City / Al Barsha South can begin repricing listings with a metro-narrative rider now.
</p>

<p style="margin: 0 0 20px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">Who is it for?</strong><br>
Brokers underwriting JVC / MBR City / Meydan / Al Barsha South / Business Bay listings, off-plan investors picking transit-oriented units before the build premium peaks, secondary sellers timing a metro-narrative reprice, and developers re-cutting feasibility for the Gold Line timeline.

<p style="margin: 0 0 16px; font-size: 15px; color: #f5f5f0; line-height: 1.6;">
<strong style="color: #f7ff00;">What is it?</strong><br>
AMIS GPD Development broke ground 2026-06-19 on Fleurs de Jardin in Meydan District 11 — a branded villa community co-created with high-jewellery house Jacob &amp; Co. featuring 5-BR villas and 6-BR grand mansions. The project is AMIS' sixth in under two years, lifting its book past AED 2B (~USD 544M) across 340+ units.

</p>
